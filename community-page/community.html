VIRTUAL READING GROUP

Would you like to join interesting people and have interesting conversations based on readings from the history of liberty?

Free Participation! | Powered by Zoom



Our VRGs will each focus on a particular topic and a common set of readings will form the basis for our discussions. Each group is facilitated by aprofessional moderator and is conducted via the Zoom online platform.

Participation is offered at no-cost. and there is no need to be an expert on the topic for discussion! The only requirement is that participants be eagerto read and engage in comversation

Participants who successfully complete all sessions will receive an e-gift certificate from Amazon.com!
